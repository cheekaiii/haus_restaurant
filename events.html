Malaysia Day Culinary Showcase

Immerse yourself in the rich and diverse flavors of Malaysia as we celebrate Malaysia Day with a spectacular culinary showcase. Join us for an unforgettable gastronomic journey that pays tribute to the country's vibrant food culture.

🗓 Date
September 16, 2023
⏳Time
6:00pm - 10:00pm
📍Venue
Haus Restaurant
💲Price
RM 129.00 per person
📢Highlights
Taste of Malaysia Buffet

Indulge in a grand buffet featuring a wide array of Malaysian dishes from various regions. From fragrant Nasi Lemak to savory Satay and spicy Rendang, experience the true essence of Malaysia's culinary heritage.


Live Cooking Stations

Watch as skilled chefs demonstrate the art of creating iconic Malaysian street food right before your eyes. Roti Canai, Char Kway Teow, and Teh Tarik are just a few of the mouthwatering delights to be showcased.


Cultural Performances

Enjoy captivating cultural performances that showcase Malaysia's diverse traditions and heritage. From traditional dances to live music, the evening will be a celebration of the nation's unity in diversity.


Flavors of Malaysia Cocktail Bar

Quench your thirst with a selection of creatively crafted cocktails inspired by Malaysian flavors. Sip on a Pandan Mojito or a Lychee Sambal Margarita as you mingle with fellow attendees.


Limited seats are available for this exclusive event.
Don't miss your chance to be part of this unforgettable showcase!
Reserve or contact 010-393 9282 to secure your spot.

Ice-Breaker Games

Themed Decor

Set up the restaurant with a thematic and cozy ambiance that creates a comfortable and inviting atmosphere. Use soft lighting, romantic decorations, and perhaps even some playful elements related to the single's theme.

Host a speed dating session where attendees have a limited time to get to know each other before moving on to the next potential match.

Keep the energy going with a dance floor and upbeat music for those who want to let loose and dance the night away.

Jazz & Bites Night:
An Evening of Musical Delights! 🎵

Get ready to immerse yourself in a soulful symphony of melodies and indulge in delectable flavors at our enchanting Jazz & Bites Night event! Join us for an unforgettable evening where the magic of live jazz music blends seamlessly with exquisite culinary creations, creating an atmosphere that tantalizes your senses and leaves you craving for more.

🗓 Date
November 5, 2023
⏳Time
6:30pm - 11:00pm
📍Venue
Haus Restaurant
💲Price
RM 89.00 per person
📢Highlights
Musical Journey

As the sun sets and the city lights begin to twinkle, our stage will come alive with the enchanting sounds of jazz. Talented musicians will take you on a captivating journey through the rich history of this iconic genre. From smooth and sultry saxophone solos to mesmerizing piano improvisations, each note will weave a tapestry of emotions that transcend time.


Culinary Delights

Satisfy your palate with a curated selection of delectable bites that have been meticulously crafted to complement the rhythm of the music. Our culinary artisans have prepared a tantalizing menu that fuses flavors from around the world, creating a symphony of tastes that mirrors the diversity of jazz itself. From savory appetizers to sweet surprises, every dish is a harmonious blend of creativity and taste.


Unwind and Socialize

Jazz & Bites Night isn't just about the music and food—it's about connecting with fellow music enthusiasts and foodies. The event offers a relaxed and cozy ambiance, perfect for mingling, striking up conversations, and forging new friendships. Whether you're a jazz aficionado or a novice eager to explore, you'll find yourself in the company of like-minded individuals who share your passion for cultural exploration.
