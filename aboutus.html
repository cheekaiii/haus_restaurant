About Us

Welcome to HAUS Restaurant, where every cup tells a story and every bite is a flavorful journey.

HAUS History

The restaurant name, HAUS was agreed and finalised through discussion among the 8 young individuals in June 2005.


The name, HAUS is pronounced like the word “House”, which often relates to the idea of home. It is HAUS priority to make the customers feel right at home and also appreciate their “home”, Malaysia.

Our Achievement

  • Michelin Star Recognition

    20th August, 2009

    HAUS Restaurant is honored to have been awarded a prestigious Michelin star, a symbol of culinary excellence.

    This accolade reflects our commitment to delivering exceptional dining experiences and pushing the boundaries of gastronomy.

  • Sustainable Dining Champion

    13th July, 2013

    HAUS Restaurant is proud to be recognized as a leader in sustainable dining.We have achieved a carbon-neutral footprint, reduced food waste, and sourced our ingredients locally, supporting both our environment and local communities.

  • Top 50 Restaurants Worldwide

    5th January, 2015

    HAUS Restaurant has been consistently ranked among the top 50 restaurants globally by renowned food critics and publications.

    Our innovative menu, exceptional service, and immersive dining atmosphere have garnered international acclaim.

  • Guest Choice Award

    27th December, 2019

    HAUS Restaurant is grateful for the unwavering support of our guests. We've received numerous guest choice awards, reflecting the satisfaction and loyalty of our diners. These awards motivate us to continue delivering memorable dining experiences.
